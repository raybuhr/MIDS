SOS Schools in South and Central America: Country List

SOS Children currently runs 26 SOS Schools, 9 SOS Nurseries and 8 SOS Training Centres in South and Central America.

21 of these SOS Schools are Primary Schools, 5 are Secondary Schools.  The SOS Nurseries often complement the work of the SOS Schools by providing the initial formal education to children from SOS Children's Villages, and other children from the community alike.  The 8 Training Centres vary in the types of vocational qualifications they provide.
