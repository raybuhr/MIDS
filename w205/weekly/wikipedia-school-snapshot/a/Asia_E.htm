SOS Schools in Asia: Country List

In Asia, SOS Children currently operates 55 SOS Schools, 65 SOS Nurseries and 25 SOS Training Centres.

Of the 55 SOS Schools that SOS Children runs in Asia, 31 are Primary Schools and 24 are Secondary Schools.  The SOS Nurseries cmoplement the work of the SOS Schools, by preparing giving children their first exposure to formal education.  The Training Centres provide vocational appreticeships and cIasses aimed at teaching certain skills or trades.  In China, SOS Children opreates its biggest SOS School in the world, in Yantai.  This school educates over 3,000 pupils.
